Three things that I feel I have a decent grasp of:


  • Growth vs Fixed mindset
  • The three-step learning process:
    1. Set the stage
      • clear the stage - eliminate distractions
      • clarify the WHAT and the WHY - what are we doing and why are we doing it?
      • prioritize process over product - a sound process will lead to a sound product. don't ignore the details
    2. Play
      • wrestle with it - it won't always be perfect out the gate, keep trying
      • take risks - no risks = no growth
      • have fun - pretty self-explanatory!
    3. Reflect
      • self-assess - review what is going well and what needs improving
      • ask new questions - if you don't know something or need help, ask someone. use your resources
      • repeat
